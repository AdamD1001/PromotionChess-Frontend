What are the rules of Promotion Chess?

Objectives:

The goal of the game is to checkmate the opposing player's king by placing the king in check (in danger of being taken) in which the opposing player has no other legal mover to escape check.


How To Play:

Each player has 15 Pawns and one king. While pawns can attack in any one space direction, pawns can only move forward (either horizontally, diagonally, or forward vertically) one space. Pawns cannot move backwards (neither vertically nor diagonally). The king can move in any direction one space. Whenever a player captures one of the opposing player's pieces, the attacking piece will be promoted to the next highest rank piece. If the opposing piece is more than two ranks higher than the attacking piece, then the attacking piece will be promoted to the piece that is one rank lower than the opposing piece.

All pieces besides the pawn move as they normally would in Chess.


Rankings: from lowest to highest
